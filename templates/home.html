Instructions

The video in the end of the page presents a demonstration of the Benchmarking Framework. The above Controller is the Framework's controller which depicts the components of the framework. The first and mandatory step is the Monitoring. This step trigger the benchmarking engine which will later record the networks' traffic. Second step is the Benchmarking Engine. In this you can select which blockchain network to configure and start, but also you can generate traffic which are transactions between random generated accounts. The managment component is the third one where you can understand the logs of the nodes and the network's information, also you can navigate to Dozzle for the complete logs and to Grafana where you can watch the actual benchmargking diagrams. The Available Networks presents the current running networks configured in previous steps.

About Blockchain Benchmarking Framework

Since the choice of a blockchain protocol is challenging, especially for corporates that seek to use the technology for their products and/or services, we developed an architecture for a blockchain benchmarking framework that aims to serve as a staged environment for supporting blockchain researchers and developers to test and validate the performance of a blockchain protocol under various settings and synthetic scenarios. In addition, the modules provided by the benchmarking framework aim to identify any performance bottlenecks during the network being under heavy load.
